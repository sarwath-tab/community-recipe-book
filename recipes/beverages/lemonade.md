## Lemonade
 
**Preparation Time:** 10 minutes  
**Serves:** 2
 
### Ingredients
- 2 lemons  
- 2 tbsp sugar  
- 2 cups water  
- A pinch of salt  
- Ice cubes (optional)
 
### Instructions
1. Squeeze lemons and extract the juice.  
2. Mix sugar, salt, and water until dissolved.  
3. Add lemon juice and stir well.  
4. Serve chilled with ice cubes.
