## Fruit Salad
 
**Preparation Time:** 15 minutes  
**Serves:** 2
 
### Ingredients
- 1 apple (chopped)  
- 1 banana (sliced)  
- ½ cup grapes  
- ½ cup pineapple pieces  
- 1 tsp honey  
- A pinch of cinnamon  
 
### Instructions
1. Wash and cut all fruits into bite-sized pieces.  
2. Mix in a large bowl.  
3. Add honey and cinnamon; toss gently.  
4. Chill for 10 minutes before serving.
