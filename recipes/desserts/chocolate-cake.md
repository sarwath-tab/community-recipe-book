## Chocolate Cake
 
**Preparation Time:** 45 minutes  
**Serves:** 4
 
### Ingredients
- 1½ cups all-purpose flour  
- 1 cup sugar  
- ½ cup cocoa powder  
- 1 tsp baking soda  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1 cup milk  
- ½ cup vegetable oil  
- 1 tbsp vinegar  
 
### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).  
2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.  
3. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.  
4. Pour into a greased baking pan.  
5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.  
6. Cool and serve with chocolate frosting.
