## Vegetable Pasta
 
**Preparation Time:** 25 minutes  
**Serves:** 2
 
### Ingredients
- 200g pasta (penne or fusilli)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup chopped onions
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, capsicum, beans)
- 1/2 cup tomato puree
- 1 tsp chili flakes
- Salt to taste
- Fresh basil or coriander (optional)
 
### Instructions
1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden.
3. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 3–4 minutes.
4. Stir in tomato puree, chili flakes, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
5. Add boiled pasta, toss well, and cook for another 2 minutes.
6. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot.
